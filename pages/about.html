About Us

Santa Fe Cafe is a step up from ordinary, fast casual dining, with a modern, open atmosphere and inventive dishes served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner 7 days a week. Grab a table at one of our comfortable indoor or outdoor seating areas, next to the beautiful fountains of Waterside Shops, or sip a delicious cup of cafe con leche or a fresh fruit smoothie at the counter. Our menu features classic American dishes, as well as Argentinean specialties. Breakfast is served until 11:30 am. Besides an extensive food menu, patrons can enjoy gourmet espressos or coffees, fresh squeezed juices, vegetable or fruit smoothies. Beer, wine, mimosas and bellinis are also available. The restaurant is run under the direction of Lucas Mautone, a long-time Naples resident and former food and beverage manager and sommelier at La Playa Beach and Golf Resort in North Naples. Santa Fe Cafe welcomes you and your family to join us while shopping at Waterside Shops or as a destination for healthy, savory American and Argentinean dishes any day of the week.
