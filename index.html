Santa Fe Cafe

OUR STORY

Enhance your shopping experience or make Santa Fe Cafe News & Espresso a destination stop for healthy American or international dishes, coffees, espressos, smoothies or freshly squeezed juices, all served in a beautiful, casual indoor/outdoor setting next door to Max Mara at Waterside Shops.

We Provide Locally Crafted Food

Santa Fe Cafe will your thrill your taste buds with classics like eggs benedict, hand-crafted hot and cold sandwiches and burgers to flavor-filled international selections like Argentinean empanadas and churrasco steak.

This Is All About Santa Fe Cafe

Open 7 days a week for breakfast and lunch, you can choose to eat in or take your meal or beverage to go. At Santa Fe Cafe it’s always easy, breezy, delectable and fun!
